#### A. "Panic vs. Recession" Framework (Quantified)

**Panic indicators** (from 2009 speech):
- Household wealth decline: >-15 to -20%
- GDP single quarter: <-6%
- Stock market annual: >-40%
- Bank market cap: >-50%

**Threshold**:

```
THEN systemic crisis = extraordinary intervention justified
```

#### C. Independence Definition (Refined)

**2010-03 Speech**:
> "The Fed is not independent from government. It is independent within government."

**5 Conditions to Earn Independence**:
1. Narrow focus on congressionally-mandated remit
2. Competent execution (especially price stability)
3. Avoid mission creep
4. Accept accountability when fail
5. Stay within liquidity provider role (not capital allocator)

**Threats**:
- External: Government pressure for loose policy
- Internal: "A little more inflation for less unemployment" trade-off
- Mission creep: "Repair shop for broken statutes"

**15-Year Consistency**: 2010 → 2025完全相同的定义和条件
